Target Audience

Our target audience is local entrepreneurs who want to reach local clients in the city and the other way around, clients who are looking for businesses that meets their needs. The reach of the Chamber of Commerce is not limited to sales or promotion of products and services. Here will also be a place where candidates may find positions to fill at different local businesses.

Personas

Persona 1

Local entrepreneur, about 40 years old. Has been 5 years working on his own business and its looking to find better ways to connect with vendors and clients in order to increase anual profit. Spends about 2 hours a day on the internet with business purposes.

Persona 2

Young man, accountings student. Looks to start his own business and to team up with local businesses and find investors for his start up

Scenarios

  • Where do I find investors for my new business?
  • How can I team up with other local businesses?
  • Where do I find training as a new local entrepreneur?
  • How do I get success as a local entrepreneur?
